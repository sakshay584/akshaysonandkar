Hello, I am Akshay.

I am a geek who loves to solve problems by building hardware. Currently I am working as a Mechanical Design Engineer in Atomionics, Singapore; designing opto-mechanics for quantum gravimeters. Before this, I was working as a Robotics Engineer in Morphle Labs, building automated pathology slide scanners. I also founded a consumer electronics startup that developed wireless solutions for indoor navigation and object tracking. I am always looking forward to collaborating on new and interesting projects to widen my perspective and learn new skills.
